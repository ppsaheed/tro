What is unique about your approach to training?

What sets us apart is our blend of traditional and modern approaches to chess training. We provide real textbooks and workbooks, adhering closely to this syllabus to guide teaching, assess progress, and support student development.

Do you provide trial classes?

We offer a free trial class for kids, giving them the opportunity to experience our online chess lessons before signing up.

Are the instructors FIDE rated and experienced?

All our chess instructors are FIDE-rated players with extensive experience teaching children. They excel at making the learning process engaging and enjoyable.

What is the class size for online sessions?

We keep our class sizes small, with typically 5 to 14 students per session, to ensure each student receives personalized attention and the instructor can focus on individual needs.

Is there a curriculum for the classes?

We follow a structured curriculum tailored to various skill levels, allowing students to advance through stages as they master fundamental and advanced chess concepts.

Vision

Mission

At Trophero chess club, our mission is to provide high-quality chess education that promotes intellectual growth, sportsmanship, and community engagement. We strive to create a supportive environment where students can learn, compete, and thrive in their chess journey.
