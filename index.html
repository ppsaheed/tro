Vision

To inspire and empower individuals of all ages to develop critical thinking and strategic skills through the game of chess, fostering a lifelong love for the game.

Mission

At Trophero chess club, our mission is to provide high-quality chess education that promotes intellectual growth, sportsmanship, and community engagement. We strive to create a supportive environment where students can learn, compete, and thrive in their chess journey.
