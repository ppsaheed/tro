The Trophero chess club, founded in 2017 in Chennai by FIDE National Instructor Survar Subair. Known for its commitment to excellence, the club has produced students who have achieved titles as National and State Champions. With over 7 years of competitive training experience, the team at Trophero is dedicated to nurturing chess talent and advancing player’s skills to achieve success at all levels.

Trophero chess club

Vision

To inspire and empower individuals of all ages to develop critical thinking and strategic skills through the game of chess, fostering a lifelong love for the game.

Mission

At Trophero chess club, our mission is to provide high-quality chess education that promotes intellectual growth, sportsmanship, and community engagement. We strive to create a supportive environment where students can learn, compete, and thrive in their chess journey.
